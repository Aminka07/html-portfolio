The best movies I have ever seen

Top 3

Interstellar (2014)

Directed by Christopher Nolan, Interstellar is a visually stunning and thought-provoking film about space exploration, time dilation, and human survival. The story follows a team of astronauts traveling through a wormhole to find a new habitable planet for humanity. The emotional depth, Hans Zimmer’s powerful soundtrack, and the mind-bending concepts of time make it unforgettable.


The Green Mile (1999)

A deeply emotional film set on death row, blending drama and fantasy.The film explores justice, racism, morality, and the power of kindness in the face of cruelty. It’s a film that stays with you long after watching, with moments of sorrow, hope, and redemption.


Deadpool (2016)

A fun, action-packed superhero movie with dark humor.The movie constantly makes fun of superhero clichés, pop culture, and even itself. The film features insane fight sequences, creative kills, and a no-holds-barred approach to violence.
